About Us

The Gleam Team has been proudly serving homes & businesses across Northeast Ohio since 2013, with a current customer base of nearly 80 residential and businesses. The company had its roots in Bad Girl Ventures, a women-run business & mentoring program, where it was one of 3 business selected from a pool of 20 to receive a grand prize standing. Since then, Luanne's Gleam Team has been committed to building on that record of excellence.


About the Founder

My name is Luanne Shifflett, owner and operator of Luanne's Gleam Team. The best way to describe my company is that we take over where other cleaning companies left off. I began cleaning homes for close friends three years ago as a side job to supplement my income... I never imagined that only a year later, I would have too many customers that it would be impossible for me to clean all of those homes by myself! I recognize that my company's reputation was built primarily from word-of-mouth from customers like you who loved our services. My team and I have been in the cleaning business for nearly 3 years now, and we owe it to you for our success. Our committment is YOUR satisfaction -- thank you for your continued support!
